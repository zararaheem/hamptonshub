Enrollment · DRI: Campus Coordinator / Admissions

Student roster

Add, edit, review, and bulk-import students for each week — and mark whether each laptop is assigned and ready. This is the source of truth that feeds the Attendance and Tech tabs. Saved to the shared database, so everyone sees the same roster.

Count by level
CSV import: works with two formats, auto-detected. (1) The Alpha Summer registration export — one row per child with a "Weeks" column: each child is created/updated in every week they attend, and parent/guardian/emergency details flow straight into Family Contacts. (2) A simple roster — First/Name, Grade, Preferred name, Birthday/DOB, Shirt size, Bus rider, Allergies, Medications, Notes, Media consent, contacts (columns matched by name, any order; imports into the selected week). Both match existing students by first name and show a review screen before anything saves. Editing is limited to admins; everyone can view.
Daily logistics · Carpool line

Dismissal

A live pickup queue. At the curb, add each car as it pulls up; inside, staff watch the line and tap Dismiss as kids head out. Updates in real time across devices.

↓Carpool line
Add to the line

Tap a student as their car pulls up — they go straight into the line. To send kids out together (siblings, carpools), tap Group, select them, then add as one car. Optionally type a car label first (e.g. "Blue SUV").

Dismissal notes & self-dismissal

Standing per-student instructions — mark who may self-dismiss (walk/bike home on their own) and add any pickup notes. These show on the line and in the picker.

Restricted · Family contacts viewers only

Family contacts

Primary parent, secondary parent, other guardians & emergency contact — one roster per week. Visible only to the people you've added to the Family contacts list (Access tab). Allergies/meds shown for context. Edit inline or import a file in the Students tab.
